Oil marked down, the Iran clock pushed out

Two weeks on, the crowd repriced crude oil lower and pushed the US–Iran peace timeline further out — while the model's sharpest disagreement with the crowd sits on a tennis court. The public ledger has grown from two open calls to ten.

1 · Crude oil marked decisively lower

The repricing we described in #005 continued — it deepened rather than reversed. In the "What will WTI Crude Oil hit in May?" event the high-price outcome ($105) shed roughly −76pp on the week to near zero, while the lower bands ($90, $95) firmed to near-certainty. The June crude event moved the same way — its higher-price outcomes lost about −40 to −47pp. We are describing where the crowd moved its money, not forecasting the barrel.

2 · The US–Iran peace clock got pushed out

Inside the "US × Iran permanent peace deal by…?" event the near-term dates collapsed: the "by May 31" outcome fell about −22pp to ~3%, and "by June 30" fell roughly −19pp to ~29%. The crowd isn't pricing an imminent deal; it is pricing delay. That sits inside a wider cluster of Iran markets now on the ledger — airspace closure, a US-invasion market, and the Hormuz contracts — which is where several of our open calls live.

3 · Where the model most disagrees: a tennis court

QEST's largest live gap this snapshot isn't geopolitics — it's the French Open. After Jannik Sinner's odds collapsed about −71pp to zero, the crowd marked Alexander Zverev up to ~38%. The transparent baseline reads him lower, at ~27% — a −10.9pp disagreement. It is logged and being scored, and it resolves soon — a falsifiable call that will be scored on the ledger either way. Not advice; a number we will be held to.

4 · The MicroStrategy conviction re-firmed

In #005 the "MicroStrategy sells any Bitcoin" lean had eased. This snapshot the nearer "by June 30" window jumped about +20pp to ~67%, and the by-year-end market sits ~85–88%. We flagged the easing; honesty means flagging the re-firming just as plainly.

  • MicroStrategy BTC sale — noted easing in #005; has since re-firmed (nearer window +~20pp). Both directions reported.

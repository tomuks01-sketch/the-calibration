SYSTEM

Three moving parts. All auditable.

No black box. The model is documented, the ledger is public, and the briefs are scored against the same record.

  1. 01

    The ledger edge

    A transparent baseline model logs every divergence from crowd pricing. Append-only, Brier-scored at resolution. Wrong calls stay forever — not deleted, not reworded.

    Scoreboard →
  2. 02

    The briefs read

    Short, regular editorial on what the crowd just repriced and why. Calls made in briefs land on the same scored ledger. Accountability, not predictions.

    Briefs →
  3. 03

    QEST model

    A documented mean-reversion baseline, named openly. Not “AI”, not an edge claim — a reproducible benchmark whose full source is in the public repo. Check it.

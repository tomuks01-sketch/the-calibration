On the bench

What gets built next

Honest roadmap. Nothing on this page is sold yet — the audience comes first, the features follow it. Listed in the order they actually make sense to build.

  1. Next

    Calibration alerts

    Email or webhook when a market diverges from the QEST baseline by >4pp, or when a tracked ledger call resolves. Replaces refreshing the terminal manually.

  2. Soon

    Saved setups

    Personal watchlists of markets you care about, with your own calibration view. Reuses the existing model output — no new scoring fabricated.

  3. Later

    Historical calibration export

    Full append-only ledger as CSV / JSON. Already public as ledger.json; this just packages it for analysts.

  4. Later

    Read-only data API

    Programmatic access to the calibrated snapshot. Built only when there are people asking for it — not before.

No pricing page yet. This product earns paid tiers when it has an audience to charge — not before. Until then everything you see is free, public, and source-visible.

Why this exists

Most prediction-market dashboards re-display the same public odds. This one adds two things you can audit. A public Scoreboard where a transparent baseline model's calls are Brier-scored the moment their markets resolve — wins and losses kept on the record forever. And short Crowd Signal briefs that describe what the crowd actually repriced, and why it mattered.

Analytics only. Public, read-only data. No predictions, no betting advice, no wallet access.

How the signal is built

Each stock's signal is a label, not a forecast, assembled only from data you can check:

  • Momentum — whether the 24h, 7d and 30d changes point the same way (rising / falling / mixed).
  • Change — price now versus the prior close, ~5 trading days, and ~1 month.

We deliberately do not publish a "chance to go up/down" number for stocks. Equity markets are highly efficient and the free, keyless data is delayed and fragile — a fabricated probability would be the opposite of useful. Our scored, falsifiable forecasts live on the Crypto page, where the data supports honest 24h reads that we log and grade in public. Market cap and derivatives are marked not shown rather than invented.

Stock Pulse is descriptive analytics of public, delayed market data. It does not predict prices, does not give financial or investment advice, and makes no profit claims. Data via a public endpoint; figures are delayed. Think for yourself.
